Pi Delta Psi Fraternity, Incorporated

Pi Delta Psi is the fastest growing Asian-American interest fraternity in the United States. Founded in the spring of 1997, the Zeta Chapter of Pi Delta Psi at New York University is home to 25+ active brothers from all backgrounds, joined by one brotherhood.

FALL 22 RUSH

Rush is a week-long series of events held at the beginning of the semester. During rush or recruitment, you have the opportunity to meet and interact with the brothers of the Fraternity. We are an Asian-interest fraternity, but we are not Asian-exclusive; we are interested in anybody interested in us.

Brotherhood
NYU Pi Delta Psi was established on May 17th of 1997 and since then, our chapter has proudly crossed over 200 brothers with many more to come. We value the lifelong bonds we have created within both NYU and beyond.
Alumni Network
Our alumni have excelled in many professional fields such as economics, finance, computer science, film, medicine, law, and beyond. We have brothers at top companies such as PwC, Shangri-La Hotels, Deloitte, and more.
Philanthropy
We have a strong commitment to community service as well as cultural awareness. The past years, we have participated in events with various non-profit organizations such as Relay for Life and The Bowery Mission.
